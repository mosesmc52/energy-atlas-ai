Frequently asked questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Plan pricing is driven from the configured billing plans in the app.

How many alerts can I have?

Your alert limit comes from the active subscription plan configured in billing.

What happens on the free plan?

Free access is controlled by the local free plan in billing, including its active alert limit.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscription status remains tied to Stripe and your local billing records.
